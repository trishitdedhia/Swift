Textbooks Remastered

Growing up, I hated textbooks. I never liked reading them because somehow I never found them immersive. I also didn't like looking back at them for last minute revisions because they were anything but simple and concise. And not to forget, filled with jargon, often black and white, and failed to explain simple concepts in a simple fashion. And so, to solve this problem, I created: Textbooks Remastered! They were crisp, simple and mimicked the peer learning experience because they were made by students themselves! All the textbooks were pre-highlighted, beautiful and explained topics in the words of learners rather than teachers!

Since this project was educational like most of my other projects, I decided to connect it with CrackBox. This also allowed us to integrate it with CrackBox Plus and other CrackBox related projects. If you don't know what CrackBox is, click here.
