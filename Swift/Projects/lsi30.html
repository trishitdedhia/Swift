Learn Something In 30mins!

Learn Something In 30mins was a series of fun and engaging crash courses my classmates and I hosted to bring together the school community in a virtual environment. I came up with the idea in 2021 beginning as a solution to just have a fun school club where students and teachers can share some of their most absurd and unique skills in a crisp 30mins session!

Later, the idea was adapted to create a season of 8 proper structured sessions. And all proceeds were directed towards covid relief; to help underprivileged communities often from the slums of Mumbai to cope with the lockdown's detrimental impact on them.
