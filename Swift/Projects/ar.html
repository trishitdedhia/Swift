AR+

When I was in 8th grade, I participated in an Inter-school Science Exhibition called MISA Synergy; MISA standing for: "Mumbai International Schools Association". My team placed first in the senior category.

Our project was an Augmented Reality mobile application made using Vuforia and Unity that augmented 2D diagrams from our textbooks into 3D animated models. It was the first time I ever worked with AR technology and the project had a personal significance to me, because 2D diagrams in textbooks was something I had often struggled with. Also, making learning fun and innovative was always something I wanted to do, and this project was a great tool for learning something or helping visual learners like me.

Unfortunately I lost this project when my laptop's motherboard had a short circuit a few years later. However, I did use the same technology for CrackBox Plus. If you want to go see CrackBox Plus click here.
