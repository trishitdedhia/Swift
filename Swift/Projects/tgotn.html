The Gift Of The Nile

I first learnt basic video editing in 6th Grade when my school introduced me to iMovie. Later that year, I challenged myself to create a documentary on the Egyptian Civilisation as part of my unit presentation in Individuals and Societies class.

I titled the documentary 'The Gift Of The Nile' because of how the entire Egyptian Civilisation flourished on the basis of the Nile. I also made a trailer!
